The Original Idea:

My original idea had nothing to do with what im currently doing, i was planning on
making a fully-automated AI enviorment that would control a drone, but since it was hard
to find a programmable drone, or even if there was, that it didn't use blocky code for it. I ended up dropping the idea after failing to succesfully make the vision AI send the
information to the index AI through text-to-speech, and other issues i won't dive into.

DcMotor Desperation:

One of the main reasons i dropped the main idea was due to wrongly provided DcMotors that didn't react to
the submitted Arduino IDE code, this, i think, because the smartcarShield v1.1 couldn't provide instructions
or direction to the motors at all, you need a module for this, then why didn't it come with the original kit?

The Rebirth

After the first project failed, i got frustrated, deeply, i was tired, i overslept, i tried so hard, all for nothing, until...
i stumbled across a 40 second video, my YT feed is filled with tech-related stuff, so it wasn't surprising when i saw
someone controlling a servo, but that's the thing, he wasn't using sketch, he was using his own hands and a camera. I looked up what library or method that was, and the rest is history.
